The Last Letter

In The Last Letter, players follow the emotional journey of Arjun and Maya, childhood friends turned lovers. With Maya diagnosed with a terminal illness, Arjun faces difficult choices as he holds her final sealed letter. Will he respect her wishes or open it early, uncovering the truth inside? This narrative game explores themes of love, loss, and impactful decisions.
